Gather Required Documents
Collect all necessary documents before visiting the bank
VIDEO
▼
🎬
Document Checklist Guide
4:15 minutes • Complete list
Required documents
  • Original Aadhaar card and a photocopy
  • Bank passbook or recent account statement
  • PAN card (if available)
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
💡 Document Tips

Ensure your mobile number is updated in Aadhaar. Carry both original and photocopies of all documents.

4
Visit Your Bank Branch
Go to your bank branch to enable DBT
VIDEO
▼
🎬
Bank Visit Walkthrough
5:20 minutes • Real bank demo
At the bank
  • Visit the branch where you opened your account
  • Ask for the DBT enablement form at the customer service desk
  • Fill the form completely with accurate information
  • Submit the form along with required documents
  • Get an acknowledgment receipt
💡 Visit Tips

Visit during working hours (usually 10 AM to 4 PM). Carry a pen and be prepared to wait in queue.

Verification Process
Understand what happens after form submission
VIDEO
▼
🎬
Verification Explained
3:00 minutes • Process timeline
Verification steps
  • Bank will verify your Aadhaar details with UIDAI
  • Your account details will be cross-checked
  • Bank will update your account status in their system
  • You may receive SMS confirmation once enabled
  • Process typically takes 2-3 working days
💡 Wait Tips

Keep your mobile phone active for SMS updates. You can check status again using our tool after 3 days.

Benefits & Next Steps

After enablement

  • LPG subsidies will be credited to your account

Monitor your account regularly for benefit credits. Contact bank if you don't receive expected benefits.
