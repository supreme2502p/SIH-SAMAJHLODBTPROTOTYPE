Aadhaar-Linked Account

An account where your 12-digit Aadhaar number is updated in the bank's Core Banking System (CBS)

What it enables:

  • ✓ Identity authentication for basic banking services.
  • ✓ Biometric authentication for transactions.
  • ✓ KYC compliance for mobile SIM activation.
  • ✓ General Aadhaar-based services.

What it :

  • ✗ Cannot receive government scholarships or subsidies.
  • ✗ Cannot receive DBT payments.
  • ✗ Only verifies your identity, nothing more.

Risk: High risk of payment delays or non-receipt of scholarship funds

DBT-Enabled Aadhaar-Seeded Account

An account that has completed two critical steps:

  1. 1. Seeding: Aadhaar number verified and stored in bank's Core Banking System.
  2. 2. NPCI Mapping: Bank uploads the Aadhaar-account mapping to the National Payments Corporation of India's Aadhaar Payments Bridge System.

What it enables:

  • ✓ Direct credit of government subsidies and scholarships.
  • ✓ Immediate DBT transfers from government schemes.
  • ✓ Automated fund disbursement without intermediaries.

What systems are updated to:

  • ✓ Core Banking System (CBS)
  • ✓ NPCI Mapper

No risk - funds flow directly once seeding is confirmed
