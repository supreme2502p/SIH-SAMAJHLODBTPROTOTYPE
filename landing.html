Aadhaar-Linked Account

An account where your 12-digit Aadhaar number is updated in the bank's Core Banking System (CBS)

What it enables:

  • ✓ Identity authentication for basic banking services.
  • ✓ Biometric authentication for transactions.
  • ✓ KYC compliance for mobile SIM activation.
  • ✓ General Aadhaar-based services.

What it :

  • ✗ Cannot receive government scholarships or subsidies.
  • ✗ Cannot receive DBT payments.
  • ✗ Only verifies your identity, nothing more.

Risk: High risk of payment delays or non-receipt of scholarship funds

DBT-Enabled Aadhaar-Seeded Account

An account that has completed two critical steps:

  1. 1. Seeding: Aadhaar number verified and stored in bank's Core Banking System.
  2. 2. NPCI Mapping: Bank uploads the Aadhaar-account mapping to the National Payments Corporation of India's Aadhaar Payments Bridge System.

What it enables:

  • ✓ Direct credit of government subsidies and scholarships.
  • ✓ Immediate DBT transfers from government schemes.
  • ✓ Automated fund disbursement without intermediaries.

What systems are updated to:

  • ✓ Core Banking System (CBS)
  • ✓ NPCI Mapper

No risk - funds flow directly once seeding is confirmed

Document Checklist Guide
4:15 minutes • Complete list
Required documents
  • Original Aadhaar card and a photocopy
  • Bank passbook or recent account statement
  • PAN card (if available)
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
💡 Document Tips

Ensure your mobile number is updated in Aadhaar. Carry both original and photocopies of all documents.

Bank Visit Walkthrough
5:20 minutes • Real bank demo
At the bank
  • Visit the branch where you opened your account
  • Ask for the DBT enablement form at the customer service desk
  • Fill the form completely with accurate information
  • Submit the form along with required documents
  • Get an acknowledgment receipt
💡 Visit Tips

Visit during working hours (usually 10 AM to 4 PM). Carry a pen and be prepared to wait in queue.

Verification Explained
3:00 minutes • Process timeline
Verification steps
  • Bank will verify your Aadhaar details with UIDAI
  • Your account details will be cross-checked
  • Bank will update your account status in their system
  • You may receive SMS confirmation once enabled
  • Process typically takes 2-3 working days

Keep your mobile phone active for SMS updates. You can check status again using our tool after 3 days.
